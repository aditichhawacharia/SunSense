Sunsense - UV Protection Tools

Personalized Sunscreen Recommender

The Personalized Sunscreen Quiz helps users find the best sunscreen for their skin type, sun exposure habits, and specific concerns through a tailored, easy-to-use questionnaire.

Vacation Plan Generator

The Vacation Plan Generator provides a personalized packing list based on the type of vacation (e.g., hiking, biking) and the season, ensuring users bring everything they need for their trip.

Sunscreen Calculator

The Sunscreen Application Calculator helps users determine the optimal amount of sunscreen to apply based on their body size, the SPF of the product, and the duration of sun exposure, ensuring effective protection.
